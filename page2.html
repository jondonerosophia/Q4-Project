NOLI ME TANGERE

Characters

Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • A wealthy young man of mixed Spanish and Filipino ancestry.
  • Ibarra is a sincere and idealistic young guy who shares his father Don Rafael's way of thinking.
  • He was born and raised in the Philippines, but he spent seven years of his childhood studying in Europe.

Maria Clara De Los Santos
  • Maria Clara is shown as a calm and demure young woman.
  • She is shown as a devout Catholic who is extremely religious.
  • Though being the biological daughter of Padre Damaso, she is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago.

Padre Damaso Verdolagas
  • The real father of Maria Clara was Padre Damaso, a former town priest in San Diego.
  • He is defined as an extrovert who is snobby, manipulative, and judgmental.

Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Crisostomo.
  • He wants to achieve justice, education, and equality for all.
  • Don Rafael helps the students with their school materials so when he died,
    a lot of them discontinued because they were ashamed.

Narcisa (Sisa)
  • A loving mother of Basilio and Crispin.
  • She went insane after both of her sons went missing.
  • She works hard for her family even though her husband is abusive and
    doesn't care about his family's well-being.

Basilio
  • He is almost 10 years old.
  • Brother of Crispin.
  • Matured and strong.

Crispin
  • He is 7 years old.
  • Brother of Basilio.
  • Coward and small.
